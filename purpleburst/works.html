Website Design

One of my strongest points is in the website design I can design all kinds of websites since nostalgic-looking ones up to modern Web 2.0 and HTML5 based sites. For my websites depending on what the customer wants I tend to write mainly in HTML and CSS if the website requires of dynamic features libraries such as JavaScript and JQuery are used as well and of course also the use of PHP and ASP.net in the case that the site requires server-side communication.

Also I know how to program websites from scratch with the help of powerful frameworks and site builders such as Wordpress, Drupal, Post-Nuke, Joomla.

If making a community site is what you are looking for I can also design full featured forums and virtual communities using top-notch scripts such as vBulletin and Invision Power Board.

Logo and Graphic Design

Aside from website design I work also in maybe one of the most important areas of the whole development process of a website, Your "image" the image that you will show to your customers through the design of logos, animations, banners and all kinds of digital media.

This is the most important step and actually before planning the development of a full featured website an identity is needed before all the technical aspects of a site, I can also cooperate on this since I have experience with the most common design tools used in the industry nowadays such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, After Effects, Flash and so on.
